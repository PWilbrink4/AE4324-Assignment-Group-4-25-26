The files in this folder were used to define and test the various functions for the kinematics of the robot,
such as the inverse kinematics, forward kinematics, and Jacobian:

DirectForwardKinematics.py
InverseKinematics.py
JacobianDef.py

Additionally, certain standalone tasks that didnt require the robot were performed, such as plotting the workspace and finding the symbolic expressions of
the different kinematics functions:

WorkspacePlotting.py
SympyForwardKinematics.py

Furthermore, it hosts tasks in preparation of runs with the real life robot, such as testing and visualisation of the trajectory:

TrajectoryTest.py
WorkspacePlotting.py
